Justice For All, Not Just For Those Who Can Afford It

Simanxebanxeba is a community-based organization dedicated to addressing injustices and ensuring equal access to justice for marginalized communities in South Africa.

Our Mission

We are committed to bridging the justice gap and creating a more equitable society

We Are Wounded and Bleeding as a Society

The name "Simanxebanxeba" is a Zulu word meaning "we are wounded and bleeding as a society." It reflects our focus on addressing the injustices of the past and the challenges facing crime fighting in modern democratic societies.

Our vision is to ensure justice for all, particularly the most vulnerable members of society who lack the means to access justice. We believe that justice should not be a privilege reserved for those who can afford it, but a fundamental right guaranteed to all.

Through legal assistance, advocacy, and community support, we strive to create a society where everyone has equal access to justice, regardless of their economic or social status.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive support to marginalized communities

Legal Assistance

We provide free or low-cost legal services to vulnerable individuals, ensuring they receive fair treatment and equal access to justice.

Court Support

We assist victims of crime with quality statement taking for court preparations and accompany them to court proceedings.

Community Workshops

We conduct workshops to educate community members about their rights, responsibilities, and the justice system.

Advocacy & Policy

We advocate for policy changes and reforms that address systemic barriers to justice, promoting a more equitable society.
